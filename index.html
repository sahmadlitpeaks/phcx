About Us

Modern medicine, delivered with a personal touch.

Precision Health Clinix was founded on a simple idea: high-quality healthcare should meet people where they live. We pair experienced clinicians with thoughtful technology to deliver care that is timely, dignified, and tailored to each patient.

From short-term recovery support to ongoing chronic care management, our team coordinates every visit, prescription, and follow-up so families can focus on what matters most.

  • Patient-first care plans designed around your goals
  • Coordinated visits between providers, nurses, and therapists
  • Transparent communication with patients and caregivers
  • Insurance-friendly billing and clear estimates

Homecare Services

Skilled care in the comfort of home.

From short-term recovery to long-term support, our clinicians bring the clinic to your door with the same standards you’d expect from a hospital outpatient program.

Skilled Nursing

Wound care, IV therapy, medication management, and post-hospital monitoring delivered by licensed RNs.

Chronic Care Management

Ongoing monitoring for diabetes, hypertension, COPD, and heart conditions with monthly clinician check-ins.

Post-Acute Recovery

Coordinated recovery plans after surgery or hospitalization to reduce readmissions and accelerate healing.

Physical & Occupational Therapy

In-home rehabilitation for mobility, strength, and daily-living skills, tailored to your environment.

Caregiver Respite & Support

Trained aides give family caregivers a break while ensuring loved ones receive attentive, dignified care.

Care Coordination

One care manager keeps your providers, pharmacy, and family aligned across every appointment.
